DESCRIPTION OF WORK:
---------------------
Flag of Wallis and Futuna, edited and corrected for proper ratio (3:2).
The French Tricolour flag has an official ratio of 3:2. However, the
French Ensign (canton) embedded in the Wallis and Futuna flag on Wikimedia
was not correctly proportioned to reflect this 3:2 ratio. This version
corrects that by:

  - Taking the Wallis and Futuna flag base layout
  - Removing the incorrect ratio of French Ensign canton
  - Replacing it with a correct ratio 3:2 French Ensign sourced
    from the flag of French Southern and Antarctic Territories (which
    contains a correct ratio French Ensign)
  - Composited and edited using Paint.NET
